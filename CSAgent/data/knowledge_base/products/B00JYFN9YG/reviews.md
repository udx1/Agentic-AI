# Customer Review Sample: ICE 100mm ND1000 Square Filter Neutral Density 10 Stop Optical Glass fits Cokin Z

- Rating 5.0/5, verified purchase: True. Glass filter produces same results as more expensive filters: I decided to test the ICE 10-stop filter against other filters that were 3 times the price. I expected to witness a noticeable drop in quality based on the price, but there was none. This glass filter has uniform density throughout and produces very suitable long exposures. There is a slight color tint when using the filter (which even the more expensive filters suffer from), but nothing that cannot be corrected in post-production. The filter lacks a foam seal like the Lee Big Stopper, but dozens of test shots during a recent trip to Iceland yielded no light leaks. For those exploring the world of long exposure photography, this filter is a great budget option.
