# Customer Review Sample: LaCie Rugged All-Terrain Safe 500 GB USB 2.0/FireWire 800 Portable External Hard Drive 301491

- Rating 4.0/5, verified purchase: True. it is very sturdy and portable in a check-in bag without damage from ...: It works exactly as described...unfortunately it works too well. I used this for my home and was the sole user, so I registered 8 fingertips. I also set the password. But should you forget your password and you didn't set your biometric print, LaCie customer support will be unable to help you. The most they can do is do a data swipe of your data and reset it to factory setting to allow you to reset your password. Besides this issue, it is very sturdy and portable in a check-in bag without damage from shock. I chose to carry only one cable, since you can power the drive and transfer data with the same cable. It's also very light weight. I didn't give it five stars because I believe the password length is limited but doesn't necessarily notify you as you type that you've entered in the maximum number of characters. So be mindful as you type your password if your passwords tend to be on the lengthy side.
