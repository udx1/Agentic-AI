Slide 2: The Problem
Every day, hospitals across the country are flying blind.

Discharge planning is reactive, not proactive. When a hospital can't predict how long a patient will stay, the consequences ripple outward: beds sit occupied past their optimal turnover point, elective procedures get delayed, nursing staff are misallocated, and revenue leaks at every seam.

The numbers are staggering. Unplanned extended stays cost U.S. hospitals an estimated $30 billion annually. The average hospital operates at only 65% bed efficiency. And with CMS penalties for readmissions tightening every year, the pressure on hospital administrators has never been greater.

The root cause? No reliable, data-driven way to predict length of stay (LOS) at the point of admission. Clinical teams rely on experience and intuition. That's no longer good enough.

---

Slide 3: The Solution
NexusHealth AI is a B2B SaaS platform that integrates directly with a hospital's existing Electronic Health Record (EHR) system to predict patient length of stay in real time — at the moment of admission, and updated continuously throughout the care journey.

Our model ingests over 200 clinical and operational variables — diagnosis codes, vitals, lab trends, care team assignments, historical comorbidity patterns — and generates a dynamic LOS prediction with a confidence interval that clinical and operational staff can actually act on.

The result: hospitals can plan discharges proactively, optimize bed management, right-size staffing, and reduce costly bottlenecks — all before they become a problem.

We don't replace clinical judgment. We sharpen it.

NexusHealth AI is built for the people who actually run hospitals — not just the C-suite.

For Bed Management Teams: A live dashboard showing predicted discharge dates and confidence scores for every inpatient, sortable by unit, acuity level, and expected LOS deviation.

For Hospital Administrators: A weekly operational intelligence report tying LOS predictions to capacity planning, staffing projections, and revenue impact estimates.
